Frequently Asked Questions
What is keyword density?+
Keyword density is the percentage of times your target keyword appears relative to the total word count. Aim for 1-2% for SEO.
What are stop words?+
Common short words like 'the', 'a', 'is', 'and' that appear very frequently but have little meaning. Usually excluded from frequency analysis.
Is there a word count limit?+
No limit. Analyse entire documents, books, or any length of text.
Is my text stored?+
