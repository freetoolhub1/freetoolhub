What is a Percentage Calculator?

A percentage calculator handles common percentage calculations instantly. Whether you need to find what percentage one number is of another, calculate a discount, or work out percentage change, our three-in-one tool covers it all.

Three Calculators in One

Calculator 1: What is X% of Y?

Use when you know the percentage and the total and want the specific amount. Perfect for calculating tips, discounts, and commissions. Example: What is 15% of 200? Answer: 30.

Calculator 2: X is what percent of Y?

Use when you know both numbers and want to express them as a percentage. Example: 45 is what percent of 180? Answer: 25%.

Calculator 3: Percentage change

Measures how much something has increased or decreased. Example: from 80 to 100 is a 25% increase.

Everyday Uses

  • Shopping discounts — calculate sale prices and total savings
  • Tax calculations — work out VAT, sales tax, or income tax
  • Grade calculations — convert test scores to percentages
  • Finance — calculate interest rates and investment returns
  • Tipping — quickly calculate 10%, 15%, or 20% restaurant tips
Frequently Asked Questions
What is a percentage?+
A percentage is a number expressed as a fraction of 100. For example 25% means 25 out of every 100.
How do I calculate 20% of a number?+
Multiply the number by 0.20. For example 20% of 150 = 30. Or use the first calculator above.
What is percentage change?+
Percentage change measures how much a value has increased or decreased relative to its original value. Formula: ((New - Old) / Old) x 100.
How do I calculate a discount?+
Use the What is X% of Y calculator. For a 30% discount on 50, enter 30 and 50 to get 15.
Can I calculate both increase and decrease?+
Yes. The percentage change calculator handles both. Positive result means increase, negative means decrease.
