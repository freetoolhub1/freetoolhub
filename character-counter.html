What is a Character Counter?

A character counter counts every individual character in your text including letters, numbers, spaces, and punctuation. It also shows characters without spaces, word count, and line count — all updating in real time as you type.

Why Character Count Matters

  • Twitter / X: 280 character limit per tweet
  • Instagram bio: 150 character limit
  • SMS messages: 160 characters per message — over this you get charged double
  • SEO title tags: Keep under 60 characters to avoid Google truncating them
  • Meta descriptions: Keep under 155 characters for full display in search results

Characters vs Words — What is the Difference?

Characters count every individual symbol including spaces. Words count groups of characters separated by spaces. For most social media platforms, character count is the limit that matters.
