Why Use a Character Counter?

Twitter has a 280 character limit. Meta descriptions should be under 155 characters. SMS messages are 160 characters. Our real-time character counter shows all platform limits at once so you never have to count manually.

Platform Character Limits

  • Twitter/X — 280 characters per tweet
  • Instagram caption — 2,200 characters

  • SMS — 160 characters per message
