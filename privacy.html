Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

What data we collect

FreeToolHub.info does not collect any personal data. All tools on this website run entirely in your browser. Files you upload (such as images) are never sent to our servers — they are processed locally on your device.

Cookies

We may use anonymous analytics cookies (such as Google Analytics) to understand how many people visit our website and which tools are most popular. These cookies do not contain any personal information.

Advertising

This website may display ads provided by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalised ads by visiting Google's ad settings.

Third-party services

The QR code generator tool uses the QR Server API (api.qrserver.com) to generate QR codes. When you use this tool, your input text is sent to that service. Please review their privacy policy at qrserver.com.
