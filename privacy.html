Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Data Collection

FreeToolHub does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data you enter into our tools. All tool functionality runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your text, images, passwords, and any other inputs never leave your device.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (page views, traffic sources). This collects anonymised usage data only. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for Google Analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

Third Party Services

The QR Code Generator uses a third-party API (qrserver.com) to render QR code images. Only the text you enter for the QR code is sent to this service — no other data is transmitted.

Contact

Questions about this policy? You can reach us via the feedback option in any tool page.
