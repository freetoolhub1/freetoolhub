Frequently Asked Questions
How is this different from the basic word counter?+
The advanced version includes unique word count, keyword density, average sentence length, Flesch-Kincaid readability score, and a full keyword frequency breakdown.
What is vocabulary richness?+
The ratio of unique words to total words. Higher ratio means more varied vocabulary. Under 40% suggests repetitive writing.
What is a good Flesch score for blogs?+
60-70 is ideal for blog posts and web content. Most adults read at this level comfortably.
Is there a word limit?+
No limit. Analyse any length of text.
Is my text stored?+
No. Everything runs in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.
