Frequently Asked Questions
How is this different from the basic word counter?+
This version adds keyword density, unique word count, average sentence length, Flesch-Kincaid readability, and top keywords.
What is a good Flesch score for blogs?+
60-70 is ideal for blog posts. 70-80 for very accessible content.
What is vocabulary richness?+
The ratio of unique words to total words. Higher ratio means more varied vocabulary.
