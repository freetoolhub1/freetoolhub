How does it highlight differences?+
Green highlights show added words. Red strikethrough shows removed words. Unchanged text is normal.
What is the maximum text size?+
No limit, but very large texts (10,000+ words) may be slow.
Is it case sensitive?+
Yes. 'Hello' and 'hello' are treated as different words.
