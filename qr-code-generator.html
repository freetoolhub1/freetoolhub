What can I use a QR code for?

QR codes are used by businesses, restaurants, event organizers, and individuals to share links, contact details, Wi-Fi passwords, and more. Anyone with a smartphone camera can scan a QR code instantly.

Common uses include sharing your website URL, linking to a menu, sharing your social media profile, or printing on business cards and flyers.
