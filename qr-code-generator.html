What is a QR Code Generator?

A QR code generator creates scannable barcodes that smartphones can read instantly with their built-in camera. When scanned, they direct users to a URL, show text, dial a number, or open an email. Our free tool creates custom QR codes in seconds.

Common Uses for QR Codes

  • Business cards — link to your website, LinkedIn, or portfolio
  • Restaurant menus — replace physical menus with a digital link
  • Marketing materials — link flyers to landing pages
  • Wi-Fi sharing — let guests connect without typing a password
  • Social media — link to Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube profiles

QR Code Best Practices

Use short URLs

Shorter URLs create less complex QR codes that scan faster. Use a URL shortener if your link is very long.

Test before printing

Always scan your QR code on both iPhone and Android before printing large quantities.

Ensure high contrast

Use dark colours on light backgrounds for best scannability. Avoid low-contrast combinations.
