What is a QR Code Generator?

A QR code generator creates scannable barcodes that smartphones can read instantly. When scanned, they direct users to a URL, show text, dial a number, or open an email. Our free tool creates custom QR codes in seconds with no signup required.

Common Uses for QR Codes

  • Business cards — link to your website, LinkedIn, or portfolio instantly
  • Restaurant menus — replace physical menus with a digital link
  • Marketing materials — link flyers and posters to landing pages
  • Wi-Fi sharing — let guests connect without typing a password
  • Social media — link directly to Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube profiles

QR Code Size and Quality Guide

  • 200x200px: Digital use only, small screens
  • 300x300px: Business cards and small print
  • 400x400px: Flyers and A5 materials
  • 600x600px: Posters, banners, and large format print

QR Code Design Best Practices

  • Always test your QR code on both iPhone and Android before printing
  • Use dark colours on light backgrounds for best scannability
  • Leave a white border around the code for reliable scanning
  • Use High error correction for printed materials
