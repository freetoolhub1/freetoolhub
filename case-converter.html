Text case types explained

UPPERCASE converts every letter to capitals — useful for headings and emphasis. lowercase converts everything to small letters. Title Case capitalises the first letter of each word — used in headings and titles. Sentence case only capitalises the first letter of each sentence, which is standard for normal writing.
