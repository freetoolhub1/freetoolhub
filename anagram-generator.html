What is an Anagram?

An anagram is a word or phrase formed by rearranging the letters of another word, using all original letters exactly once.

Famous Anagrams

  • listen → silent, enlist, tinsel, inlets
  • earth → heart, hater, rathe
  • cinema → iceman
  • astronomer → moon starer
  • the eyes → they see
