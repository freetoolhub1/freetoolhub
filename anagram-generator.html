Frequently Asked Questions
What is an anagram?+
An anagram is a word formed by rearranging all the letters of another word. 'Silent' is an anagram of 'listen'.
How does the generator work?+
It checks your letters against a built-in dictionary of 500+ common English words to find all valid anagrams.
Why are some anagrams missing?+
The built-in word list covers common English words. Very rare or technical words may not be included.
What is the maximum word length?+
