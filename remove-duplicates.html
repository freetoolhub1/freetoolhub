What is a Duplicate Line Remover?

A duplicate line remover scans through any list or block of text and removes lines that appear more than once, leaving only unique entries. Also known as a deduplication tool or unique line extractor.

Common Use Cases

  • Email lists — remove duplicate addresses before sending campaigns
  • Keyword lists — deduplicate SEO keyword lists for content planning
  • Data cleaning — remove duplicate rows from exported spreadsheet data
  • URL lists — clean up link collections before bulk operations
  • Name lists — deduplicate registration data or contact lists

How to Remove Duplicate Lines

  1. Paste your list into the left input box
  2. Set your options: ignore case, trim spaces, remove empty lines
  3. Click Remove Duplicates
  4. The clean list appears in the right box instantly
  5. Click Copy Result to copy to your clipboard

Options Explained

Ignore case

Apple, apple, and APPLE are treated as duplicates when this is enabled.

Trim spaces

Removes extra spaces from line beginnings and ends before comparing.

Remove empty lines

Removes blank lines from the output for a cleaner result.
