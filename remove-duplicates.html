What is a Duplicate Line Remover?

A duplicate line remover scans through any list or block of text and removes every line that appears more than once, leaving only unique entries. Also known as a deduplication tool or unique line extractor.

When Do You Need to Remove Duplicates?

  • Email lists — remove duplicate addresses before sending newsletter campaigns
  • Keyword lists — deduplicate SEO keyword lists for content planning
  • Data cleaning — remove duplicate rows from exported spreadsheet data
  • URL lists — clean up link collections before bulk operations
  • Name lists — deduplicate registration data or contact databases

How to Remove Duplicate Lines

  1. Paste your list into the left input box
  2. Set your options: ignore case, trim spaces, remove empty lines
  3. Click Remove Duplicates
  4. The clean deduplicated list appears in the right box instantly
  5. Click Copy Result to copy to your clipboard

Deduplication Options Explained

Ignore case

Apple, apple, and APPLE are treated as duplicates. Useful for email lists where capitalisation varies.

Trim spaces

Removes leading and trailing spaces before comparing, so "apple " and "apple" are correctly identified as duplicates.

Remove empty lines

Removes blank lines from the output for a clean result.
