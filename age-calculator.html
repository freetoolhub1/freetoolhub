What is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator determines your exact age from your date of birth, accounting for leap years and the varying lengths of months. Unlike simply subtracting years, our tool calculates your precise age in years, months, and days.

How to Calculate Your Exact Age

  1. Enter your date of birth using the date picker
  2. The reference date defaults to today — change it if needed
  3. Click Calculate My Age
  4. View your exact age plus total months, weeks, and days
  5. See how many days until your next birthday

Beyond Years: Months, Weeks and Days

Our calculator shows your age in multiple formats. Knowing you have lived 14,600 days is far more interesting than just knowing you are 40. Use it to discover interesting age milestones.

Fun Age Facts and Milestones

  • The average human lives approximately 4,000 weeks
  • At age 1 you have lived about 8,760 hours
  • People born on February 29 only have a "true" birthday every 4 years

Who Uses an Age Calculator?

  • Job applications requiring age eligibility verification
  • Medical forms needing precise age in years and months
  • Legal documents requiring exact age verification
  • Genealogy research calculating ages from historical records
Frequently Asked Questions
How accurate is this?+
Exact to the day, accounting for leap years and varying month lengths.

Yes. Enter any date of birth and any reference date.
Can I see my age in months or days?+
Yes. Results show total months, total weeks, and total days.
Does it work for past or future dates?+
Yes. Change the reference date field to any past or future date.

The calculator uses the exact calendar date. If your birthday has not occurred yet this year it shows last year's count.
