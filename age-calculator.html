How does this age calculator work?

Enter your date of birth and today's date. The calculator will work out your exact age in years, months, and days. It also shows your age in total months, weeks, and days, plus how many days until your next birthday.

This is useful for filling in forms that ask your exact age, checking if you meet age requirements, or just satisfying your curiosity.
