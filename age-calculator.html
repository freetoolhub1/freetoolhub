What is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator determines your exact age based on date of birth and a reference date. Unlike simply subtracting years, our tool calculates your precise age in years, months, and days, accounting for varying month lengths and leap years.

Who Uses an Age Calculator?

  • Job applications — verify age eligibility requirements instantly
  • Medical forms — accurately record patient age in years and months
  • Legal documents — verify minimum age requirements precisely
  • Birthday countdowns — find out exactly how many days until your next birthday
  • Genealogy research — calculate ancestor ages from historical records

How to Calculate Your Age

  1. Enter your date of birth using the date picker
  2. The reference date defaults to today — leave it or change it
  3. Click Calculate My Age
  4. View your age in years plus total months, weeks, and days
  5. See exactly how many days until your next birthday

Fun Age Facts

  • The average human lives approximately 4,000 weeks
  • At age 1 you have already lived through about 8,760 hours
  • People born on February 29 only have a true birthday every 4 years
Frequently Asked Questions
How accurate is this age calculator?+
Calculates your exact age to the day, accounting for leap years.
Can I calculate someone else's age?+
Yes. Enter any date of birth and any target date to calculate exact age between two dates.
Can I see my age in months or days?+
Yes. Results show total months, total weeks, and total days in addition to years.
Does it work for past or future dates?+
Yes. Change the reference date field to any past or future date.
Why might my age differ from what I expect?+
The calculator uses the exact calendar date. If your birthday has not occurred yet this year, it shows the previous year's count.
