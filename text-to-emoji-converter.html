Frequently Asked Questions
How does the text to emoji converter work?+
It matches every word in your text against a large dictionary of 300+ words and replaces matching words with the most relevant emoji automatically.
Can I control how many emojis are added?+
Yes. Light adds an emoji after every second matching word. Medium adds one after every matching word. Heavy wraps matching words with emojis on both sides for maximum effect.
Why are some words not converted?+
Only words that have a clear emoji equivalent are converted. Very common short words like "the", "a", "of" are not converted as there is no clear emoji match.
Is it good for Instagram captions?+
Yes. Perfect for Instagram captions, tweets, Facebook posts, and WhatsApp messages.
Does it work on mobile?+
Yes. Fully responsive and works perfectly on all smartphones and tablets.
