Frequently Asked Questions
What is GPA?+
Grade Point Average — a number (usually 0-4.0) representing your average academic performance.
Do credits affect GPA?+
Yes. Higher-credit courses have more weight. A 4-credit course affects GPA twice as much as a 2-credit course.
What is a good GPA?+
3.5+ is very good (Dean's List). 3.0+ is good. Below 2.0 is often academic probation.
