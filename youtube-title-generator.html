What is a YouTube Title Generator?

A YouTube title generator creates click-worthy, SEO-optimised video titles based on your topic or keyword. Our free tool uses proven high-CTR title formulas used by top YouTube creators to help your videos rank higher in search and get more clicks.

Getting your YouTube title right is one of the most important factors in video success. Studies show that titles with numbers get 36% more clicks, and titles that create curiosity or urgency dramatically outperform generic ones.

Why Your YouTube Title Matters

  • YouTube SEO — the title is the strongest ranking signal for YouTube search
  • Click-through rate — a compelling title gets 3-5x more clicks from search results
  • Suggested videos — YouTube recommends videos with strong keyword-title match
  • Thumbnail pairing — your title and thumbnail work together to convert viewers

Proven YouTube Title Formulas

The Number Formula

"10 [Topic] Tips That Will [Result]" — numbered lists consistently get higher CTR because viewers know exactly what to expect.

The Secret Formula

"The Secret [Topic] Nobody Tells You" — creates curiosity and implies exclusive insider knowledge.

The Mistake Formula

"[Number] [Topic] Mistakes You're Making (And How to Fix Them)" — speaks directly to pain points and promises a solution.

The How-To Formula

"How to [Achieve Result] in [Timeframe]" — the most searched format on YouTube. Direct, clear, and keyword-rich.

YouTube Title SEO Tips

  • Keep titles under 60 characters to avoid truncation in search results
  • Put your main keyword as close to the beginning as possible
  • Use brackets [Like This] or parentheses (Like This) for bonus context — they increase CTR
  • Never clickbait — if the title promises something, the video must deliver it
  • Test multiple titles using YouTube's A/B testing feature after publishing
