Why You Need a Strong Password

Over 80% of data breaches are caused by weak or stolen passwords. A short password can be cracked in seconds, while a 16-character password with mixed characters takes millions of years to crack by brute force. Our free generator creates genuinely random, cryptographically secure passwords every time.

How Our Password Generator Works

Our tool uses your browser's built-in random number system to create completely random passwords. The password is created entirely in your browser — nothing is ever transmitted to our servers or stored anywhere.

Password Length and Complexity Guide

  • 8 characters, letters only: cracked in seconds
  • 12 mixed characters: takes months to crack
  • 16 mixed with symbols: takes thousands of years
  • 20+ mixed with symbols: effectively uncrackable

Password Security Best Practices

  • Use a unique password for every single account
  • Enable two-factor authentication on all important accounts
  • Store passwords in a reputable password manager
  • Change passwords for critical accounts every 6-12 months

How to Store Generated Passwords Safely

Never write passwords in plain text files or sticky notes. Use a dedicated password manager that encrypts your passwords. Bitwarden is free and open-source. 1Password and Dashlane are excellent paid options.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is it safe to use an online password generator?+
Yes. Passwords are generated entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server or stored anywhere.
How long should a strong password be?+
At least 12-16 characters. Use 20+ for critical accounts like banking and email.
Should I use symbols?+
Yes. Adding symbols dramatically increases password strength and makes brute-force cracking practically impossible.
Can I reuse passwords across accounts?+
Never reuse passwords. If one account is breached, attackers try the same password on every other site.
How do I store generated passwords safely?+
Use a trusted password manager like Bitwarden (free), 1Password, or Dashlane to store passwords securely.
