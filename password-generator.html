Why use a strong password?

Weak passwords are the number one cause of account hacking. A strong password should be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols.

Our password generator creates truly random passwords in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server — your password is 100% private.
