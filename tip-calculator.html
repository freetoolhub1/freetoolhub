What is a Tip Calculator?

A tip calculator works out how much to tip at a restaurant, bar, or any service — and splits the total bill evenly among your group. No mental maths needed.

How Much Should You Tip?

  • 10% — Minimum for basic service
  • 15% — Standard for good service
  • 18% — Above average service
  • 20% — Excellent service (most common in the US)
  • 25%+ — Exceptional or to reward outstanding service

How to Split the Bill

Enter the total bill, select your tip percentage, enter the number of people in your group, and click Calculate. The tool shows exactly how much each person owes including their share of the tip.

Frequently Asked Questions
Do I tip before or after tax?+
In the US, it is standard to tip on the pre-tax amount, though tipping on the total is also acceptable and becoming more common.
How is the tip split calculated?+
The tip is added to the bill total and then divided equally by the number of people. Each person pays the same amount.
Can I enter a custom tip percentage?+
Yes. Click the Custom button and enter any percentage you want.
What currencies does this work with?+
It works with any currency — just enter the amount in your local currency. The calculator shows the symbol $ but the maths works for pounds, euros, or any other currency.
