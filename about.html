ABOUT FREETOOLHUB

We built FreeToolHub because free should actually mean free — no accounts, no paywalls, no data collection. Just tools that work.

Why We Built This
The problem with most online tools

Most free online tools are not really free. They make you create an account, show you 10 ads per page, upload your files to their servers, limit how many times you can use them, or hide the best features behind a paywall. We were tired of that — so we built FreeToolHub.

Every single tool on FreeToolHub runs entirely in your browser. Your text, images, and passwords never leave your device. No account needed. No limits. No tricks.
