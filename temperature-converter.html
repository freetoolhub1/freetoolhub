Temperature Reference Points

  • Absolute zero: -273.15°C / -459.67°F / 0K
  • Water freezes: 0°C / 32°F / 273.15K
  • Room temperature: 20-22°C / 68-72°F
  • Body temperature: 37°C / 98.6°F

Frequently Asked Questions
What units are supported?+
Celsius (°C), Fahrenheit (°F), Kelvin (K) and Rankine (°R).
What is the formula for Celsius to Fahrenheit?+
°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32. For example: 100°C = 212°F.
What is Kelvin?+
Kelvin is the SI unit of temperature starting from absolute zero (-273.15°C). Used in science and physics.
