What is a Password Strength Checker?

A password strength checker analyses your password and tells you how secure it is against common hacking methods. Our free tool checks length, character variety, patterns, and common vulnerabilities — giving you an instant strength rating and specific improvement tips.

How Hackers Crack Passwords

  • Brute force attacks — trying every possible combination. Short passwords fall in seconds.
  • Dictionary attacks — trying common words, names, and phrases. Never use real words.
  • Credential stuffing — using leaked passwords from other breaches. Never reuse passwords.
  • Rainbow table attacks — using pre-computed hash databases. Complex passwords are immune.

Password Strength Levels Explained

Very Weak (0-20%)

Under 6 characters, no variety. Cracked instantly. Change it immediately.

Weak (21-40%)

Short or single character type only. Cracked in minutes. Not acceptable for any account.

Fair (41-60%)

Some variety but too short or predictable. Improve before using for important accounts.

Strong (61-80%)

Good length with variety. Suitable for most accounts with 2FA enabled.

Very Strong (81-100%)

Long, complex, unique. Would take millions of years to crack. Ideal for all accounts.

Frequently Asked Questions
How does the password strength checker work?+
It analyses your password across 6 factors: length, uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, special symbols, and common password patterns. Each factor adds to your overall strength score.
Is my password safe to enter here?+
Yes. The checker runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your password is never sent to any server, never stored, and never logged anywhere. It is 100% private.
What makes a password strong?+
A strong password is at least 12 characters long and includes uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols. It should not be a real word, name, or predictable pattern.
What is a good password strength score?+
We rate passwords from Very Weak to Very Strong. Aim for Strong or Very Strong for all accounts. Any password rated Weak or Very Weak should be changed immediately.
How long would it take to crack my password?+
The checker estimates crack time based on current computing power. A 12-character password with all character types would take millions of years to crack by brute force.
