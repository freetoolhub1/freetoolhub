What is an Image Compressor?

An image compressor reduces the file size of an image without noticeably affecting visual quality. Our free tool works entirely in your browser — nothing is ever uploaded to any server, making it 100% private and instant.

Why Image Compression Matters for SEO

Google's Core Web Vitals directly measure page loading speed. Images are typically the largest files on any webpage. Compressing them helps you:

  • Improve your Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) score
  • Reduce total page weight by 50-80%
  • Rank higher on Google due to better PageSpeed scores
  • Reduce mobile data usage for visitors

How to Compress an Image Online

  1. Click Choose Image or drag your JPG or PNG into the upload area
  2. Use the quality slider — 75% is recommended for web use
  3. See the live preview comparing original vs compressed size
  4. Click Compress and Download to save your smaller image

Understanding Quality Settings

90-100% — Minimal compression

Very little size reduction. Use for professional printing or when absolute quality is required.

70-85% — Recommended for web

File size drops 50-75% with virtually no visible quality loss.

50-70% — High compression

Good for thumbnails or low-bandwidth situations where size is the priority.

Frequently Asked Questions
