How image compression works

Image compression reduces the file size of an image by removing unnecessary data. JPEG compression works by reducing colour detail in areas the human eye is less sensitive to. A quality setting of 70–80% gives a great balance between small file size and good visual quality.

Smaller images load faster on websites and use less storage. This tool is perfect for optimising images before uploading to your website or social media.
