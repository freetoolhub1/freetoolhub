What is a Word Counter?

A word counter is a tool that instantly calculates the total number of words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs in any text. Whether you are a student checking an essay length, a blogger optimising content for SEO, or a writer hitting a deadline, our free tool gives accurate results in real time.

Who Should Use This Tool?

  • Students — verify essay word counts before submitting
  • Bloggers — optimise article length for SEO rankings
  • Social media managers — stay within character limits
  • SEO specialists — keep meta descriptions under 160 characters
  • Writers — track daily writing progress and chapter length

Key Features

Real-time counting

No button to press. The moment you type or paste text, all four statistics update simultaneously.

Four statistics at once

Most tools only show word count. Ours shows words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs all at once.

No word limit

Paste an entire book chapter or a 5,000-word article — our counter handles it all instantly.

How to Use the Word Counter

  1. Click inside the text box above
  2. Type or paste your text using Ctrl+V (Cmd+V on Mac)
  3. Watch all four stats update in real time
  4. Use Copy to copy text or Clear to start fresh

SEO Word Count Tips

  • Blog posts: aim for 1,500-2,500 words for best Google rankings
  • Meta descriptions: keep under 160 characters
  • Twitter/X posts: 280 character limit
  • LinkedIn posts: best engagement under 1,300 characters
