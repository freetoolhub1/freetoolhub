What is a Word Counter?

A word counter calculates the total number of words, characters, sentences and paragraphs in any text instantly. Our free tool updates in real time as you type — no button press needed. Perfect for students checking essay length, bloggers optimising content for SEO, and professionals working to tight deadlines.

Who Should Use This Tool?

  • Students — verify essay word counts before submitting assignments
  • Bloggers and writers — optimise article length for Google rankings
  • Social media managers — stay within platform character limits
  • SEO specialists — keep meta descriptions under 155 characters
  • Authors — track daily writing progress and chapter length

Key Features of Our Word Counter

Real-time counting

No button needed. All four statistics update the moment you type or paste — words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs simultaneously.

No word limit

Paste an entire novel chapter, a 10,000-word report, or a single sentence — the counter handles it all with zero slowdown.

How to Count Words Online

  1. Click inside the text box above
  2. Type directly or paste with Ctrl+V (Cmd+V on Mac)
  3. Read your stats instantly — no button to press
  4. Use Copy to save your text or Clear to start fresh

Word Count Tips for SEO and Writing

  • Blog posts between 1,500 and 2,500 words rank best on Google
  • Meta descriptions: keep under 155 characters to avoid truncation
  • Twitter posts: 280 character maximum
  • LinkedIn posts get best engagement under 1,300 characters
  • YouTube descriptions: first 200 characters are most important
