What is a Word Counter?

A word counter is a tool that counts the total number of words, characters, sentences and paragraphs in a piece of text. It is used by writers, students, bloggers and content creators to check the length of their work.

Our free online word counter updates in real time as you type. It also counts characters with and without spaces, making it useful for social media posts, essays, and SEO content writing.
