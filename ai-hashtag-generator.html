Frequently Asked Questions
How many hashtags should I use on Instagram?+
Instagram recommends 3-5 hashtags, though up to 30 are allowed. Mix large, medium, and niche hashtags for best reach.
Do hashtags still work in 2026?+
Yes, hashtags still help content get discovered, especially on Instagram and TikTok. Relevance is more important than quantity.
Should I use the same hashtags every time?+
No. Rotate your hashtags to avoid being flagged as spam by Instagram. Use a different set for each post.
What is the best hashtag size?+
Mix hashtags with 10k-100k posts (niche), 100k-1M posts (medium), and 1M+ posts (large) for the best reach balance.
