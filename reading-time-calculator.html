Average Reading Speeds

  • Slow reader: 100-150 wpm — children or reading in second language

Frequently Asked Questions
What is the average reading speed?+
The average adult reads 200-250 words per minute for non-technical content. Technical content averages 100-150 wpm.
Does it account for images?+
It calculates based on text only. Add approximately 10 seconds per image to your estimate manually.
What is a good reading time for a blog post?+
7 minutes (around 1,500-1,800 words) is the sweet spot — long enough to be valuable, short enough to keep readers engaged.
Is there a word limit?+
No limit. Analyse any length of content.
