What Should a Professional Invoice Include?

  • Invoice number — unique identifier for tracking and tax purposes
  • Your business details — name, address, email, and payment details
  • Client details — who you are billing
  • Issue date and due date — when it was issued and when payment is expected
  • Line items — description, quantity, rate, and amount for each service
  • Subtotal, tax, and total — clearly broken down
  • Payment terms — Net 30, Net 15, or due on receipt
