How to resize an image

Upload your image by clicking or dragging it into the box above. Enter the new width or height in pixels — if "Lock aspect ratio" is on, the other dimension will update automatically to keep the image proportions correct.

Click "Resize & Download" and your resized image will download immediately. No data is uploaded to any server — everything happens in your browser.
